<p> Official vanguard gaming center terms of service

By joining and participating in this server, you agree to follow all rules listed below. Failure to comply may result in punishments as outlined.
Punishment System
All punishments follow a structured 

escalation system unless stated otherwise:
Levels:
Warning
Final Warning
Mute / Kick / Ban (depending on severity)
Some violations may skip steps depending on severity.

1. Respect Everyone
Do not engage in bullying, harassment, or toxic behavior.
Punishments:
1 hour mute + final warning
Immediate ban (severe cases)

2. English Only
All chats must be in English to keep communication clear.
Punishments:
1 hour mute + warning
1 day mute + final warning
Kick

3. Use the Correct Channels
Post content only in appropriate channels (no misplaced ads/messages).
Punishments:
Warning
Final warning
1 day mute
4. No Spamming
Do not spam or repeatedly send the same messages.
Punishments:
Warning
1 hour mute
Kick

5. No Real-Life Trading
All trades/sales must be done in-game only.
Punishments:
1 day mute + warning
Ban

6. One Ad Per Item
Do not repost the same ad—update your existing one instead.
Punishments:
Warning
1 hour mute
1 day mute

7. No Scams or Fake Deals
Scamming or fake listings are strictly forbidden.
Punishment:
Immediate ban (with proof)

8. Respect Staff Decisions
You may share opinions respectfully, but do not argue excessively with staff decisions.
Punishments:
Warning
1 day mute
Kick

9. Use the Correct Voice Channels
Use voice channels for their intended purpose (e.g., music only in music channels).
Punishments:
Warning
1 day mute
Kick

10. No NSFW Content
No pornography, gore, or offensive content.
Punishments:
1 day mute
1 week mute
Ban

11. No Metagaming
Keep roleplay realistic and within RP logic.
Punishments:
1 day mute
1 week mute
Ban

12. Appropriate Nicknames
No offensive, trolling, or impersonating names.
Punishments:
1 week mute
Ban

13. No Server Advertising
Promoting other servers without permission is forbidden.
Punishments:
1 week mute
Ban

14. Stay Civil in Deals
Disagreements must remain respectful. Report issues instead of arguing.
Punishments:
1 day mute
1 week mute
Kick

15. Report Suspicious Behavior
If you notice suspicious activity, tag staff using @ to resolve the issue.

16. Restricted Areas (Vault Rule)
Robbing the Vanguard Vault is strictly forbidden.
Punishments:
Final warning + compensation required
Ban
17. Giveaway Rules
All giveaways must be announced in advance:
Small giveaways: at least 3 days prior
Large giveaways: at least 7 days prior <p>
